Dubai Real Estate Insights

Everything you need to know about investing and living in Dubai.

Does buying property entitle me to a residence visa?

Yes. As of 2026, sole owners of property qualify for a 2-year Investor Visa. Investments of AED 2 million or more (including off-plan) qualify for the 10-year Golden Visa.

Do I have to pay tax on my property in Dubai?

No. Dubai does not levy annual property tax, personal income tax, or capital gains tax on residential property, making it one of the most tax-efficient markets in the world.

Is my off-plan investment protected?

Yes. By law, all developers must register projects with RERA and use dedicated, DLD-approved Escrow accounts for all buyer payments, ensuring funds are strictly used for construction.
